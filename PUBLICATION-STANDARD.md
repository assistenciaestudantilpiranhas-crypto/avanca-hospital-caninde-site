# PUBLICATION-STANDARD.md — Public Website Publication Standard

This document defines publication rules for the Avança Hospital Canindé public website.

This repository follows GHAES — Global Health AI Engineering Standard.

Reference:
https://github.com/erickgomesal/ghaes

## 1. Publication Purpose

The website must be suitable for:

- public presentation;
- institutional review;
- technical communication;
- parliamentary amendment support;
- project law support;
- municipal health management communication;
- portfolio presentation.

## 2. Professional Tone

Public text must be:

- technical;
- institutional;
- clear;
- objective;
- verifiable;
- respectful;
- free of informal development notes.

Do not include expressions that suggest the content was generated by AI.

## 3. Evidence and Claims

Do not publish:

- numbers without source;
- laws without verification;
- political claims without approval;
- funding claims without institutional basis;
- clinical claims as if they were official protocols unless approved.

## 4. SEO and Metadata

Preserve and review:

- title;
- description;
- canonical URL;
- Open Graph title;
- Open Graph description;
- Open Graph image;
- favicon;
- robots.txt;
- sitemap.xml.

## 5. Netlify Safety

Do not change Netlify deployment settings unless explicitly approved.

Avoid redirect rules that can break static assets such as:

- CSS;
- JavaScript;
- SVG;
- favicon;
- images.

## 6. Final Report Requirement

Every publication-related task must end with:

1. Summary
2. Files changed
3. Public content impact
4. SEO impact
5. Accessibility impact
6. Deployment impact
7. Validation performed
8. Risks
9. Pending decisions
10. Commit/push status
